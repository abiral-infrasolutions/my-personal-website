Our Business

abiral-infrasolutions Pvt. Ltd. is established with the primary objective of supporting national infrastructure growth and development, including but not limited to:

  • Planning, design, construction, maintenance, and development of infrastructure projects such as roads, bridges, buildings, hydropower, telecommunications, water supply, and irrigation.
  • Providing consulting, project management, and engineering services in civil and related sectors.
  • Supplying, trading, and leasing of construction materials, machinery, and equipment.
  • Engaging in any other legal business activities as permitted by Nepali law relevant to infrastructure and engineering development.

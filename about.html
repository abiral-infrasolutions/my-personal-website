About Us

Legal Profile & Company Objectives

abiral-infrasolutions Pvt. Ltd. (previously Abiral Infra Solutions Pvt. Ltd.) is a registered private limited company, incorporated under the Companies Act of Nepal in November 2020 (2077 BS). The company’s registered office is located in Bagmati Province, Kathmandu Metropolitan City, Ward No. 3, Kathmandu District, Nepal.

Our business is legally recognized as both commercial and entrepreneurial in nature, and we operate in full compliance with relevant Nepali laws and regulations.

Our Business Scope

abiral-infrasolutions Pvt. Ltd. was established to drive innovation and progress in Nepal’s infrastructure sector. The company’s core objectives and activities include:

  • Planning, development, design, and implementation of major infrastructure projects such as hydropower, highways, mechanical and electrical engineering works, buildings, roads, bridges, water supply, irrigation, telecommunications, and more.
  • Supplying and trading of machinery, equipment, raw materials, vehicles, generators, agricultural and household goods, electrical and construction materials—sourced locally or imported as per legal requirements.
  • Providing engineering consulting, project management, technical supervision, and operational support across various sectors including construction, real estate, energy, and public utilities.
  • Engaging in import/export, distribution, dealership, stockist activities, and supply chains related to infrastructure and development materials.
  • Participating in any other lawful business activities that support infrastructure, industrial, environmental, and technological development as permitted by prevailing Nepali law.

abiral-infrasolutions Pvt. Ltd. is committed to supporting the nation’s development goals by delivering innovative, reliable, and sustainable infrastructure solutions. Our management and technical team consists of experienced professionals in engineering, consulting, and project management.

Message from the Chairman

Nearly a decade ago, our team accepted the challenge of reviving this company. We faced significant financial, legal, and operational hurdles, but through hard work, patience, and collective commitment, we achieved a remarkable turnaround.

This journey was never guaranteed. It was the support of shareholders, guidance from advisors, and the dedication of our board and staff that made recovery possible. We now have a renewed responsibility to strive for excellence and set a benchmark for others facing adversity.

Yet, our journey is far from over. At 25, the company is ready for new ambitions and growth. We will look forward—embracing honesty, transparency, and ethical standards, while fostering an inclusive, safe, and empowering environment for all.

I pledge to all stakeholders: we will do everything necessary to make NHPC a respected leader in clean energy, delivering value for Nepal and our shareholders. Your continued support and guidance will remain vital as we embark on this new chapter.

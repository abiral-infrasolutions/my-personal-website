Products & Services

What We Offer

abiral-infrasolutions Pvt. Ltd. provides comprehensive infrastructure, engineering, consulting, and technology solutions to both public and private sectors. Our core products and services include:

  • Civil Engineering Services:
    • Planning, design, estimation, survey, supervision, and implementation of infrastructure projects (buildings, roads, irrigation, hydropower, water supply, sanitation, bridges, transmission lines, and more).
    • Project management and contract administration.
  • Environmental and Geospatial Solutions:
    • Environmental impact assessments and management plans.
    • Geospatial analysis and mapping (GIS, remote sensing).
    • Disaster risk assessment and mitigation planning.
  • Architectural and Urban Development:
    • Architectural design and master planning.
    • Urban and regional planning.
  • Surveying & Data Acquisition:
    • Topographical, engineering, hydrographic, and cadastral surveys.
    • Drone surveys, 3D mapping, and digital modeling.
  • Construction and Supply:
    • Turnkey construction solutions for public and private projects.
    • Procurement and supply of construction materials, technical equipment, and innovative construction products.
  • Research, Training, and Capacity Building:
    • Applied research in engineering, environment, and geospatial fields.
    • Professional training, workshops, and skills development programs.
  • Consulting and Advisory:
    • Technical, legal, and managerial consulting for infrastructure projects.
    • Feasibility studies, due diligence, and monitoring & evaluation.
  • IT & Software Services:
    • Development and deployment of engineering, mapping, and project management software.
    • Digital transformation and IT-enabled solutions for infrastructure management.
